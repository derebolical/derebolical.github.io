About

We are a team of knowledgeable engineers, technologists, and project management professionals that are dedicated to delivering high-quality and cost-effective engineered solutions to all of our industrial clients and customers throughout BC.

We provide E/I&C engineering design and consulting services for industrial retrofit and multidisciplinary greenfield projects in the Pulp & Paper, Oil & Gas, Chemical Processing, and Renewable Energy industries.

As a small firm, we always go the extra mile to ensure that our clients’ requirements are satisfied and expectations exceeded. We are knowledgeable, experienced, and committed to successfully completing every project on-time, and within budget.
